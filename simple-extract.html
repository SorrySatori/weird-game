Simple Sprite Extractor

Instructions:

  1. Click "Load FungalPriestSprite" to load the sprite
  2. The tool will automatically extract the middle character from the top row
  3. Click "Make Background Transparent" to remove the background
  4. Right-click on the extracted image and select "Save Image As..." to save it
  5. Save it as "SingleFungalPriest.png" in your assets/images directory
